Setting the Record Straight: There's More to EDI than Integration with Dynamics AX

Much has been made about products that integrate EDI data with Dynamics AX. Just walk the floor of any Microsoft Dynamics event and you will probably come across slogans like “EDI made simple” or “our product drastically simplifies EDI.”

These catchphrases make for good marketing, but they can lead customers to neglect what is fundamentally important in EDI: managing trading relationships. So, it’s time to set the record straight. EDI is about much more than integration with your ERP; it’s about securing the pipelines that carry your transactions with customers and suppliers. Ultimately, it is the lifeblood for many businesses.

NAV Tech Tip

Struggling with time-consuming and costly invoicing that has to be sent out to your customers via EDI? Manually entering data to create these invoices? Noticing errors in the transfer from NAV to your EDI documents? Getting chargebacks as a result of the above struggles?

Fear not!

You can easily create your outbound 810 (invoice) with just two clicks. Here’s how:

1) When viewing a sales order in NAV, click on the "Post" button.

2) Click on "OK" to invoice or ship and invoice.
